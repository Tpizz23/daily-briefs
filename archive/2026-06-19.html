Sermon Review

"Mending the Mess in Your Marriage" · Made For Relationships · Clayton King

TODAY'S FOCUS: Practical Steps to Rebuilding

KEY SCRIPTURE

1 Peter 4:8-10 — "Above all things, love covers a multitude of sins, since love is perfected with one another — that you may have confidence and not be ashamed before Him at His coming."

INSIGHT

Peter gives a radical command: love covers sins. Not keep records of them, not bring them up in every argument, not broadcast them to others — cover them. This is the path to mending the mess. Practical steps include: stop bringing up the past, pray together daily (even briefly), serve one another without being asked, and choose to speak kindly even when frustrated. These aren't just marriage tips — they're gospel responses.

TODAY'S APPLICATION

Commit to one concrete act of service for your spouse today that they haven't asked for. Let it be your answer to the question "How can I cover this with love?"

CLOSING PRAYER

Lord, I want to love in a way that covers sin rather than exposes it. Show me practical ways to serve my spouse today, especially in areas I've been keeping score. Teach me to extend the same grace You have extended to me. Amen.
