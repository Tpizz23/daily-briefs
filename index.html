"Mending the Mess in Your Marriage" · Made For Relationships · Clayton King

TODAY'S FOCUS: The Freedom of Surrender

KEY SCRIPTURE

Romans 12:18 — "If it is possible, as much as it depends on you, live in peace with all people."

INSIGHT

Surrender doesn't mean passivity — it means releasing the need to control the outcome. "As much as it depends on you" is an active phrase. Do everything in your power to pursue peace, and then trust God with the rest. When you've done your part — loved, served, covered, forgiven — you can rest in the truth that God is the ultimate restorer. Freedom comes when you stop carrying what God never asked you to carry.

TODAY'S APPLICATION

What have you been carrying that God is asking you to release? Make a conscious choice today to surrender that specific thing to Him — your spouse's attitude, your marriage's timeline, or your need for things to look a certain way.

CLOSING PRAYER

God, I surrender what I've been holding too tightly. I release my need for control and trust Your timing for my marriage. Help me do my part and leave the results to You. Give me peace that doesn't depend on circumstances. Amen.
